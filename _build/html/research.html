Research Work#

PhD Researcher | Ruhr University Bochum#

Research Focus: Designed an end-to-end deep reinforcement learning (RL) solution to solve a complex resource allocation bottleneck for a real-world industrial control system. My approach features a Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) agent trained via a sophisticated curriculum learning strategy to handle sparse rewards and conflicting business objectives like throughput and safety.

Key Contributions: - Developed a hybrid system combining RL with predictive modeling for operational safety and efficiency - Integrated a low-latency, inference-time planning module using XGBoost models trained on Monte Carlo simulation data - Contributed to ‘ContainerGym,’ a reusable, open-source benchmark to accelerate the development of ML solutions for industrial AI - Implemented proactive resource contention management to prevent costly system failures

Technical Skills: PyTorch, Weights & Biases, Deep Reinforcement Learning, Curriculum Learning, Monte Carlo Simulation, XGBoost, Industrial AI
